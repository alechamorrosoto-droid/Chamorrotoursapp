Payment & cancellation policy
A 50% deposit is required to confirm this booking; the remaining balance is paid on the day of the tour. If you cancel 48 hours or more before your tour, your deposit is fully refunded. If you cancel between 24 and 48 hours before your tour, the deposit is non-refundable, but you may reschedule to another date or switch tours at no extra cost. If you cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, the deposit is forfeited and cannot be rescheduled or exchanged. If we cancel your tour due to weather or sea conditions, it will be rescheduled or switched at no cost or penalty to you.
